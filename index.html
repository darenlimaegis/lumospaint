Limewash Feature Wall Singapore — Artisan Mineral Finishes

Lumos Paint is Singapore's leading limewash specialist. We create stunning limewash and Venetian stucco feature walls for homes across Singapore — a completely unique finish that no wallpaper or regular paint can replicate.

What is Limewash Painting?

Limewash is a natural mineral paint made from slaked lime. Applied in multiple thin layers, it creates a soft, matte, slightly textured finish that shifts beautifully with natural light. Unlike regular paint, no two limewash walls ever look exactly the same.

It's breathable, eco-friendly, anti-bacterial, and has been used in European homes for centuries. In Singapore, it's become the most-requested specialty wall finish — particularly for living room and bedroom feature walls.

"We've had customers say their limewash feature wall is the single thing guests comment on most. It elevates the entire room."
